# Imperatives

## Sentence Construction

- Aff: **Verb** + complement
  - E.g: **Open** the door.

***

- Neg: **Do + not** **(Dont't)** + verb + complement.
  - E.g: **Do not** open the door

***

**Exemples:**

- **Open** your mouth and say 'Aaaah'
- **Come on**, boys.
- Don't **lose** my key
- Don't **forget** to take your passport with you
- **Have** a piece of cake
